## ūüŹôÔłŹ what is this

Pump City turns the Solana token market into a living, breathing city skyline. every token gets its own building. the market moves, the city moves with it.

no more staring at red and green numbers. now you stare at red and green *skyscrapers*.

## ūüĒĎ features

**the skyline**
- every Solana token = its own building
- **big volume ‚Üí big tower.** small cap coins get little starter homes. blue chips get penthouses.
- **green roof = mooning ūüüĘ** / **red roof = dumping ūüĒī** ‚ÄĒ readable at a glance from anywhere in the city
- live ticker scrolling across the skyline so you never miss a move

**the views**
- `‚úąÔłŹ first-person` ‚ÄĒ fly through the city street-level like you own the place
- `ūüĒ≠ orbital` ‚ÄĒ zoom out and survey the whole market from above

## ūüŹóÔłŹ how the buildings work

```
building height  ‚Üí  token volume (24h)
roof color       ‚Üí  price direction (green up / red down)
building width   ‚Üí  market cap
glow intensity   ‚Üí  price change % magnitude
```

the city updates in real time as the market moves. if a token rugs, the building crumbles. if something moons, watch that tower shoot up.
